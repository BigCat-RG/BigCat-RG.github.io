Prospective Graduate Students

We are currently recruiting additional students who are interested in persuing a PhD and reserach career in AI technology and Human-AI collaboration or teamwork. If you are interested, please send an email that explains how your research interests align with BIG CAT and includes your CV. These emails can be sent over to Dr. Chris Flathmann: cflathm@clemson.edu.

Interested Collaborators

We are always looking to expand our research collaborations on funded or just interesting work surrounding AI technology. If you are have a project you'd want to work with us on or if you find some of our ongoing work interesting, feel free to email Dr. Chris Flathmann with potential points of collaboration: cflathm@clemson.edu.

Anyone Else

If you don't fit the above two categories but are still interested in learning more about what we do, feel free to reach out. We are always happy to chat more about our work, goals, and impacts on AI in society. Feel free to email Dr. Chris Flathmann: cflathm@clemson.edu.
